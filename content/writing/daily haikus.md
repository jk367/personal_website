**May 27, 2025** 

A new sun rises

streams of light cover the day 

All things possible 

**May 26, 2025** 

nest in my window

no egg, no hatchling, no sign

there was no goodbye 

**May 25, 2025**  

sinking feeling 

dead at night, window closed 

morning comes again
